About Our Company

As an Event Management and Wedding Planner company, HUSH LUSH EVENTS AND WEDDING PLANNER is your one-stop-shop for wedding planning and event coordination. We take the headache out of trying to choose the best professionals and develop an event that everyone will remember. Creating extraor- dinary events is our specialty using innovative designs, entertainments, and customized theme decor. We mainly deliver resonance to the WOW factor, while saving our client's valuable time and money.

Vision

At Hush Lush, we’re passionate about delivering extraordinary event planning and decoration services tailored to your unique vision. Unlike other planners, we don’t believe in one-size-fits-all packages because no two weddings should ever be the same.

Mission

At Hush Lush, our mission is to provide exceptional expertise in managing every aspect of your event, from the initial concept to its flawless execution. We are committed to delivering events that leave a lasting impression and are cherished as a unique experience.

What we do

We, at Hush Lush Event Management Company, specialize in planning and executing all types of events, including corporate events, weddings, birthday parties, photography, catering, choreography, and entertainment. Our professional team at Hush Lush Wedding Planners is dedicated to managing every detail to match the couple's vision, with a hands-on approach to ensure a seamless experience.

We offer a range of affordable, high-quality services for pre-wedding, wedding, and post-wedding functions, tailored to make your special moments unforgettable.
